## 框架概述

AIGF（AI Governance Framework）是一套面向企业高管与董事会的 **AI 战略落地方法论**。

核心命题：**在 AI 快速发展的不确定性中建立确定性锚点，将组织改造成"AI 战略执行机器"。**

框架采用 **4 层级 × 5 维度** 矩阵结构，生成 20 个独立但相互关联的执行模块。每个模块回答同一组核心问题：*为什么做、做什么、怎么做*，并配有路线图、评估工具、关键指标与检查清单，可直接用于 AI 治理委员会的议题决策。

### 设计原则

- 🧭 **问题导向** — 每份方案直击高管决策痛点，避免停留在概念层面
- 🗂️ **结构化视角** — 4×5 矩阵形成系统化认知地图，覆盖完整决策链条
- ⚙️ **落地执行** — 所有工具可直接用于 AI 治理委员会的议题决策

## Overview

AIGF (AI Governance Framework) is a structured **AI strategy execution methodology** for enterprise executives and boards.

Core premise: **Establish certainty anchors amid the rapid and uncertain development of AI, and transform the organization into an "AI strategy execution machine."**

The framework uses a **4-layer × 5-dimension** matrix to produce 20 independent but interrelated execution modules. Each module answers the same set of questions — *why, what, and how* — and ships with roadmaps, assessment tools, KPIs, and checklists ready for direct use in AI governance committee agendas.

### Design Principles

- 🧭 **Problem-driven** — Every playbook addresses specific executive decision pain points, avoiding concept-only discussions
- 🗂️ **Structured perspective** — The 4×5 matrix forms a systematic cognitive map covering the complete decision chain
- ⚙️ **Execution-ready** — All tools can be used directly in AI governance committee agenda items

---

## The 4×5 Matrix

Each cell is identified by a two-letter code: **Dimension (column) + Layer (row)**, e.g. `CG` = Change × Governance.

|  | **C · Change** | **V · Value** | **O · Organization** | **R · Risk** | **F · Fulfillment** |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **S · Strategy** | `CS` 01 · AI Strategic Transformation | `VS` 05 · AI-Driven Enablement | `OS` 09 · Leaders & Advocates | `RS` 13 · Strategic Risk | `FS` 17 · Strategy Execution Loop |
| **G · Government** | `CG` 02 · AI Change Management | `VG` 06 · AI Innovation Patterns | `OG` 10 · AI Governance Committee | `RG` 14 · AI Risk Management | `FG` 18 · Governance Execution Playbook |
| **O · Operation** | `CO` 03 · Operational Model Transition | `VO` 07 · Measurable AI Metrics | `OO` 11 · AI Operations Team | `RO` 15 · AI Operational Risk | `FO` 19 · Operations Execution Plan |
| **T · Technology** | `CT` 04 · Technology Roadmap Migration | `VT` 08 · AI Applicability Selection | `OT` 12 · Technical Talent Readiness | `RT` 16 · Security & Domestic Tech | `FT` 20 · Technology Execution Plan |

> **Note:** The F-column playbooks (17–20) represent **closed-loop iterative execution logic** — structurally distinct from the 16 parallel modules — each mapping to a PDCA cycle at its respective layer.
